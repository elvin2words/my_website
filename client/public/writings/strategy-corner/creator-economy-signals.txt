# Creator Economy Signals Worth Tracking in 2026

The strongest opportunities are not random trends. They are repeated signals.

Three categories to monitor:

- distribution friction and platform shifts
- conversion quality across content formats
- durability of audience trust over reach spikes

Signal quality, not vanity metrics, should shape quarterly content bets.
