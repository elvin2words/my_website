# Portable Energy Stack for Field Teams

When the work happens outside controlled environments, power strategy must be explicit.

Key design constraints:

- variable weather windows
- mixed device profiles and charging standards
- limited replacement cycles

This framework defines battery tiers, charging cadence, and monitoring checkpoints so field teams stay operational through long deployment cycles.
