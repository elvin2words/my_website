# Site Commissioning Checklist for Hybrid Microgrids

This draft captures a repeatable sequence for commissioning:

- pre-energization verification
- control logic validation
- staged load handoff
- post-handover telemetry baseline

The goal is to reduce last-minute rework and preserve commissioning quality under schedule pressure.
